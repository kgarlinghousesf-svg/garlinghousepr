About

I'm Katy Garlinghouse

I've always believed the best communications work is really just great storytelling. The technology changes, the market evolves, the jargon multiplies, but what makes a brand break through is always the same: a clear, honest, human story told at exactly the right moment.

Over 15 years I've brought that instinct to some of the most complex narratives in enterprise tech, SaaS, and cybersecurity. I've built programs from scratch at Series A startups and scaled global communications at public companies, and I've learned that the fundamentals are the same at every stage: know your audience, earn their attention, and give them a reason to care.

I'm based in the San Francisco Bay Area and work with companies that are ready to stop being the best-kept secret in their category.

  • Enterprise SaaS & cybersecurity domain expertise
  • Tier-1 media relationships across tech, business, and trade press
  • Analyst relations that earn influence without just buying it
  • Executive coaching and media training
  • Agency and in-house team leadership
  • AI tooling to scale content output without adding headcount
  • Crisis preparedness and rapid-response counsel
